SRE

There’s a good checklist on Rachel By The Bay, the gist is:

  • Rollbacks should work. On every deployment.
  • A to AB to B (a.k.a. make before break):
    • v2 code/data should not break v1 code/data
  • Strict(er than JSON) schema-ing when you fling data across places
    • Protobuf, etc.
    • You should only begin to consider JSON if you need to talk to browsers
  • Please fix 500s.
    • Also you should only get 400s when talking to external things out of your control

Her follow-up is a nice scary story too. Also reminder that “infra-as-code” abstractions isn’t an excuse not to learn the underlying infra properly.
